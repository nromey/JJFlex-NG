# Audio Presets

Audio presets let you save and recall complete audio configurations — levels, routing, and equalization — with a single action.

## What Gets Saved in a Preset

An audio preset captures your current audio settings including gain levels, headphone and line out volumes, and routing configuration.

## Using Presets

You can manage audio presets from the Audio Workshop (press `Ctrl+J` then `T` to open it). From there you can:

- **Save** your current settings as a new preset
- **Load** a saved preset to restore those settings
- **Delete** presets you no longer need

## When to Use Presets

Presets are handy for different operating situations:

- **Ragchewing:** Comfortable audio levels, maybe a bit of NR
- **Contest:** Tighter filters, different headphone balance
- **DX:** Higher gain, more aggressive noise reduction
- **CW:** Narrow filter, APF enabled, different audio routing
