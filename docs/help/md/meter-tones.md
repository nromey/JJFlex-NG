# Meter Tones

Meter tones give you audio feedback for signal strength and other meters — no need to look at a visual meter.

## How Meter Tones Work

When meter tones are enabled, JJFlexRadio converts meter readings into audio tones. The pitch rises and falls with the meter reading, giving you an intuitive sense of signal strength, power output, or whatever meter you're monitoring.

## Toggling Meter Tones

Press `Ctrl+J` then `M` to toggle meter tones on or off. You'll hear a confirmation earcon and speech announcement.

## Reading the S-Meter

Press `Ctrl+Shift+S` to hear the current S-meter reading spoken as a number (like "S7" or "20 over 9").

## Uses for Meter Tones

Meter tones are especially useful for:

- **Antenna tuning** — Listen to the tone pitch as you rotate a beam or adjust an antenna tuner. Higher pitch means stronger signal.
- **CW pileup positioning** — Find where stations are calling by listening to the tone as you tune.
- **Signal peaking** — Fine-tune frequency or antenna for maximum signal.
- **Contest operation** — Quick audio feedback without breaking your rhythm.
