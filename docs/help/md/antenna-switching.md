# Antenna Switching

JJ Flexible Radio Access lets you select which antenna your radio uses for receive and for transmit. If you have multiple antennas connected to your radio, you can switch between them without leaving the keyboard.

## Using the Menus

Open the **Slice** menu and go to the **Antenna** submenu. You will find two submenus under it:

- **RX Antenna** — choose which antenna the radio receives on.
- **TX Antenna** — choose which antenna the radio transmits on.

The antennas listed match your radio's actual antenna ports (ANT1, ANT2, XVTR, and so on), so what you see depends on your radio's model and connections. A checkmark next to an entry indicates the currently selected antenna.

When you pick an antenna, you will hear a confirmation — for example, "RX antenna ANT1" or "TX antenna ANT2."

## Using ScreenFields

You can also switch antennas from the ScreenFields panel:

1. Press `Ctrl+Shift+5` to jump to the Antenna category.
2. Use the `Up` and `Down` arrow keys to cycle through the available antennas.

Switching from ScreenFields is often the fastest way to change antennas if you are already working with other ScreenFields settings.

## Tips

- On most radios, the RX and TX antennas default to the same port. You can set them independently if you want to receive on one antenna and transmit on a different one (common for setups with a separate receive antenna like a beverage or a loop).
- The available antenna ports depend on your radio model. A FLEX-6700 has more antenna ports than a FLEX-6300, for example.
