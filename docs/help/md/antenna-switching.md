# Antenna Switching

JJ Flexible Radio Access lets you select which antenna your radio uses for receiving and transmitting. If you have multiple antennas connected, you can switch between them without leaving the keyboard.

## Using the Menus

Go to Menu > Antenna, and you'll find two submenus:

- **RX Antenna** — choose which antenna to receive on.
- **TX Antenna** — choose which antenna to transmit on.

The antennas listed match your radio's actual antenna ports (ANT1, ANT2, XVTR, etc.), so what you see depends on your radio model. A checkmark indicates the currently selected antenna.

When you pick an antenna, you'll hear confirmation: "RX antenna ANT1" or "TX antenna ANT2."

## Using ScreenFields

You can also switch antennas from the ScreenFields panel:

1. Press `Ctrl+Shift+5` to jump to the Antenna category.
2. Use `Up` and `Down` arrows to cycle through available antennas.

This is often the fastest way to switch if you're already working with other ScreenFields.

## Tips

- On most radios, the RX and TX antenna default to the same port. You can set them independently if you want to receive on one antenna and transmit on another.
- The available antenna ports depend on your radio model — a FLEX-6700 has more options than a FLEX-6300, for example.
